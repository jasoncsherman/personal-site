A sample field note. Every tool you adopt is a small recurring tax — setup, upkeep, the cost of remembering it exists. The good ones pay that tax back many times over; the rest quietly drain attention.

My rule of thumb lately: a new tool has to prove it earns its keep within a month, or it goes.
